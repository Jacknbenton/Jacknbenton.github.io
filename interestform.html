JOIN THE TEAM!

We welcome all students to play rugby for NCSU. We have two teams to accomadate for all levels of experience, meaning everyone will get a chance to practice and play. Over the history of the club, some of our best leaders and players have come from zero experience. Some backgrounds of players we see translating well to rugby is soccer, baseball, wrestling, football, basketball, and track. Our only requirement is for you to be committed to the team by coming to practices and games. It's a great chance to make lifelong friends in a sport that you will be addicted to.

Interest Form
